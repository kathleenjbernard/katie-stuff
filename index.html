About me

Hi! I'm Katie, self-taught developer and former special education teacher. Creating tools that inspire and help others reach their goals has been my passion since I was a kid.

I first became acquainted with coding as a teacher as a means to connect with my academically gifted students, many of whom spent their breaks and lunches building games or customizing characters. This grew into a passion that I then decided to pursue professionally.

I'm currently freelancing and especially enjoy building for small businesses, artists, entreprenuers, women, and people of color. Do you have a passion project, side hustle, or start up in need of an affordable website? Let's talk!

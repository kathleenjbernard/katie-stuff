About me

Hi! I'm Katie, self taught developer and former special education teacher. Creating tools that inspire and help others reach their goals has been my passion for as long as I can remember.

I first became acquainted with coding from a desire to create fresh learning challenges for my students, many of whom were gifted (and often bored with typical approaches to learning). This grew into a passion that I eventually decided to pursue full-time.

Some may say labeling myself as "self taught" may not be favorable. As a former teacher and current developer, I have an unusual set of skills that give me a unique voice and perspective. A surprising amount of what I learned while teaching has translated to web development, including expertise in data-driven goals, strong communication skills, open-mindedness, and a sense of humor.

I enjoy work that pushes traditional boundaries and helps grow small businesses.

I also have a passion for design - specifically, the place where visual art and technology intersect to create unique user experiences.

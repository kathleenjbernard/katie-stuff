About me

Creating tools that inspire, motivate, and help others has been my passion for as long as I can remember.

As a former teacher and current developer, I have an unusual set of skills that give me a unique voice and perspective. A surprising amount of what I learned while teaching has translated to web development, including expertise in data-driven goals, strong communication skills, open-mindedness, and a sense of humor.

I enjoy work that pushes traditional boundaries and helps grow small businesses.

I also have a passion for design - specifically, the place where visual art and technology intersect to create unique user experiences.
